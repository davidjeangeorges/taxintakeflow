Please read this letter carefully, and sign at the bottom to confirm your agreement before we begin preparation of your tax return.

Client Responsibilities
1
You agree to provide all documents, records, and information necessary to prepare your return, including but not limited to: W-2s, 1099s, prior year return, Social Security cards, and any other relevant documents requested by your preparer.
2
You confirm that all information provided in your intake form and any subsequent documents is accurate, complete, and truthful to the best of your knowledge.
3
You agree to respond promptly to requests from your preparer for additional information or clarification. Delays in providing information may result in delays in completing your return.
4
You are responsible for reviewing your completed return before signing and filing. Your signature on Form 8879 (e-File Authorization) represents your confirmation that the return is accurate and complete.
5
You agree to pay all fees as outlined in the fee section of this letter prior to, or at the time of, return delivery.
Our Responsibilities
1
We will prepare your federal and state income tax returns based on information you provide. We will not audit or independently verify the information you provide unless specifically engaged to do so.
2
We will apply applicable tax laws and regulations in effect for Tax Year — to minimize your tax liability within the bounds of the law.
3
We will maintain the confidentiality of all information you provide. We will not disclose your information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law.
4
We will notify you of any missing documents or questions that arise during preparation before completing your return.
5
We will provide you with copies of your completed return for your records. We will retain copies of your return for a minimum of three (3) years.
Fees & Payment

Tax preparation fees are based on the complexity of your return and the services required. Your preparer will provide a fee quote after reviewing your documents.

Payment is due upon delivery of your completed return. We accept payment via cash, check, credit/debit card, and payment from your refund (where applicable). We reserve the right to withhold your completed return until payment is received.

If you are expecting a refund, you may have the option to have fees deducted from your refund. Your preparer will discuss this option with you if available.

Limitations & Disclaimers

Our work does not constitute an audit, review, or compilation of financial statements. We are engaged solely to prepare your income tax returns based on information provided by you.

If you receive a notice from the IRS or a state tax authority after your return is filed, please contact us immediately. Responding to tax notices is not included in this engagement unless separately arranged. Additional fees apply for audit representation, IRS correspondence, and amended returns.

We are not responsible for penalties or interest resulting from inaccurate or incomplete information provided by you, or from your failure to meet tax payment deadlines.
