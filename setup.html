Connect Your URLs
Paste the two URLs that power your intake system. This is the only technical step.
Apps Script URL
This connects your intake form to Google Drive and Google Sheets. You get this URL from Google Apps Script after deploying your backend.
How to get this URL: Open script.google.com → open your TaxIntakeFlow project → click Deploy → Manage Deployments → copy the Web App URL.
GHL Inbound Webhook URL
This connects form submissions to GoHighLevel so contacts are created automatically and your workflows fire.
How to get this URL: In GHL → Automation → Workflows → TIF — New Intake Received → click the Webhook trigger → copy the Inbound Webhook URL.
ℹ️
You only ever do this once per firm. After saving, the intake form, engagement letter, and dashboard all configure themselves automatically from these two URLs. You never open any code file.
